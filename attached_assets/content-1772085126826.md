# Built on Trust.   Backed by Action.

At LoKat ("we", "our", or "us"), your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it when you use the LoKat mobile app or website.

Read on to learn more.

## Privacy Policy

1. **Data We Collect**


1. Personal Information


   - Name, email, phone number

   - Optional profile info you provide


2. Location Data


   - Real-time GPS location, only with your permission

   - Background location access for live photo requests


3. Media & Content

   - Photos and videos shared through the app

   - Request details and chat messages (encrypted)
4. Device & Usage

   - Device type, operating system, unique IDs

   - App activity, crash logs, and diagnostics

2. **Why We Collect Data**





We collect data to:

   - Operate core app features (e.g. photo request, live location)

   - Match users in real time based on location

   - Ensure safety and prevent abuse

   - Improve app functionality and performance

   - Comply with legal obligations

3. **Data Sharing**





We never sell your personal data. We may share information with:

   - Service providers (e.g., cloud storage, payment processors)

   - Other users, only to complete a request

   - Authorities, if required to comply with law or protect safety

4. **Data Sharing**





You’re always in control:

   - Location Access: Can be turned off anytime in device settings

   - Notifications: Fully customizable

   - Account Deletion: Request at support@lokat.ai

5. **Data Security**





We use encryption, secure cloud services, and internal access controls to protect your data. While no system is perfect, we actively monitor for threats and misuse.


6. **Children’s Privacy**





LoKat is not intended for users under 13. If we learn a child is using the app, we will delete their data and deactivate the account.


7. **Third-Party Links**





Our app may link to external services (e.g. maps, payments). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those platforms.

8. **Changes**





We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. All updates will be posted at lokat.ai/privacy. Continued use of the app means you accept any changes.

Questions? Reach out to: support@lokat.ai
